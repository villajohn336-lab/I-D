Weekly Work Overview

Week 1 ▼

Website Development

1. An HTML template was downloaded as the base for the website.

2. The team presentation content was developed.

3. The folder structure was carefully organized to store images, code, and resources.

4. The code was implemented in Visual Studio Code, adapting the template.

Hydration Problem Analysis

1. The hydration problem among university students was identified.

2. Ideas were shared using a collaborative Excel file.

3. Possible solutions were proposed based on collected data.

Week 2 ▼

Critical Factors Research

1. Work teams gathered to identify critical hydration factors.

2. A survey was designed to evaluate importance levels.

3. Statistical analysis was performed (boxplot, mean, normalization, hypothesis testing).

4. Conclusions were drawn based on data results.

Solution Development

Brainstorming was conducted to generate solutions.

A reusable filtered water bottle was proposed.

A sketch was created and later modeled in Fusion 360.
